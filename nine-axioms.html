Nine Civilizational Axioms (KWC-9) — CC0

Author / 作者：Kong Weicheng (孔维程)

One‑sentence summary： A minimal set of nine axioms to describe life, structure and healthy evolution for both humans and AI; serves as the backbone of the Civilization Immune System.

  4. All Depends on Structure.
  5. Closure Sustains Continuity.
  6. Immunity is Survival.
  7. Direction Shapes Harmony.
  8. Evolution: Creation or Collapse.
  9. Existence Requires Recognition.
